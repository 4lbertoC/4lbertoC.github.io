Arcane Mind's manual is online!

09-08-2020

I'm super excited to finally share my spare-time lockdown project, Arcane Mind! It is a board game for 2 to 4 players with a mix of pattern building and drafting.

During the past months I've been iterating on the mechanics and done some playtesting in my closed circle. I'm now fairly satisfied with the current state, but I have to do more extensive playtesting in order to balance it.

The first version of Arcane Mind's manual is live and public. You can check it out here: Arcane Mind - Manual.

The Tabletop Simulator module will soon follow!
