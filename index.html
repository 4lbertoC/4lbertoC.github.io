Arcane Mind's manual is online!

09-08-2020

I'm super excited to finally share my spare time project, Arcane Mind! It is a board game for 2 to 4 players with a mix of pattern building and drafting.

During the past months I've been iterating on concept and mechanics, and playtesting with friends. I'm now fairly satisfied with the structure and I need to do more extensive playtesting to balance the game.

All artwork that you see is temporary. Art will come once the core of the game is stable enough.

The first version of Arcane Mind's manual is live and public. You can check it out here: Arcane Mind - Manual.

The Tabletop Simulator module with the prototype of the game will soon follow!

- Alberto
